About

Designed as a community-driven organization, Sirius DAO empowers its members to collectively shape the future of decentralized finance, governance, and digital assets. By utilizing Constellation's scalable and secure infrastructure, SiriusDAO aims to revolutionize how decentralized autonomous organizations (DAOs) operate, fostering a more connected and efficient ecosystem for all participants. Join Sirius DAO to be part of the next evolution in decentralized governance and digital innovation.

How to get involved

Engage with the Sirius DAO community by joining their social media channels, forums, and discussions. Participating in these platforms allows you to connect with other members, ask questions, and stay updated on the latest developments. Sirius DAO has a native token, acquiring it is necessary to participate in governance or other aspects of the project. These tokens can be purchased on supported exchanges or earned through participation in the DAO’s activities.
